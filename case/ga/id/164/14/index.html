ESPEY et al. v. VILLAGE OF NORTH ATLANTA et al.
21819.
(218 Ga. 429)
(128 SE2d 489)
(1962)
GRICE, Justice.
Injunction. DeKalb Superior Court. Before Judge Hubert.
It appearing from statements made in oral argument before this court that, since the sustaining of the general demurrer to the petition and the denial of the only relief sought, to wit, an injunction, the defendants have done all that was sought to be enjoined, and no supersedeas having been granted, all issues have thus become moot; therefore, the writ of error is dismissed. Carlton v. Seaboard Air-Line R., 139 Ga. 692 (77 SE 1128).
T. B. Higdon, McCready Johnston, contra.
William F. Lozier, for plaintiffs in error.
ARGUED OCTOBER 9, 1962 -- DECIDED OCTOBER 10, 1962 -- REHEARING DENIED OCTOBER 22, 1962.
