ATLANTA NEWSPAPERS, INC., v. THE STATE OF GEORGIA, by WEBB, Solicitor-General.
20602.
(215 Ga. 325)
(110 SE2d 344)
(1959)
CANDLER, Justice.
Contempt. Fulton Superior Court. Before Judge Pye. June 22, 1959.
Under the ruling by this court in Atlanta Newspapers, Inc. v. Grimes, ante, the Court of Appeals and not this court has jurisdiction of the instant case
Paul Webb, Solicitor-General, Carl Copeland, J. Walter LeCraw, contra.
B. P. Gambrell, W. Glen Harlan, John E. Dougherty, Harold N. Hill, Jr., for plaintiff in error.
SUBMITTED SEPTEMBER 17, 1959 -- DECIDED SEPTEMBER 17, 1959.
