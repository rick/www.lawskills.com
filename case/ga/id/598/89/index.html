AIR SERVICE COMPANY v. LOVETT.
47983.
(129 Ga. App. 185)
(198 SE2d 910)
(1973)
BELL, Chief Judge.
Action on note. Clayton State Court. Before Judge Foster.
In this suit on a note in which the plaintiffs motion for summary judgment was denied, an examination of the pleadings and the evidence shows that there is a genuine issue of material fact as to the pleaded defense of lack of consideration and whether the note was executed in payment of or as security for an antecedent obligation of a third party for which no consideration is necessary. See Code Ann. 109A-3--408.
Albert B. Wallace, for appellee.
Adolphus B. Orthwein, Jr., for appellant.
ARGUED MARCH 5, 1973 -- DECIDED JUNE 8, 1973.
