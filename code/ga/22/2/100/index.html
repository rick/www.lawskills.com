Title 22, Chapter 2, Section 100 (22-2-100)

As used in this article, "condemning body" or "condemnor" means:

(1) The State of Georgia or any branch of the government of the State of Georgia;

(2) Any county or municipality of the State of Georgia;

(3) Any housing authority;

(4) Any other political subdivision of the State of Georgia which is vested with the power of eminent domain; and

(5) All other persons possessing the right or power of eminent domain.
