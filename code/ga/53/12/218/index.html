Title 53, Chapter 12, Section 218 (53-12-218)

If any part of the principal consists of land from which merchantable timber may be removed, the receipts from taking the timber from the land shall be allocated in accordance with paragraph (3) of subsection (a) of Code Section 53-12-211.
