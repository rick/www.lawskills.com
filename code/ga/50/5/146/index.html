Title 50, Chapter 5, Section 146 (50-5-146)

Any official, officer, or employee of the state who causes state property having a value of less than $200.00 to be disposed of in violation of Code Section 50-5-141 or 50-5-142 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor. If such property has a value of $200.00 or more, he shall be guilty of a felony and, upon conviction thereof, shall be punished by imprisonment for not less than one year nor more than five years.
