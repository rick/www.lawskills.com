Title 14, Chapter 9, Section 802 (14-9-802)

On application by or for a partner, the court may decree dissolution of a limited partnership whenever:

(1) It is not reasonably practicable to carry on the business in conformity with the partnership agreement; or

(2) A general partner has been guilty of such misconduct as tends to affect prejudicially the carrying on of the business.
