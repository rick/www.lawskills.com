Definitions for "subscriber" in Georgia State Code
1. (14-2-140) Title 14, Chapter 2, Section 140
(27) "Subscriber" means a person who subscribes for shares in a corporation, whether before or after incorporation.
2. (33-9-2) Title 33, Chapter 9, Section 2
(4) "Subscriber" means an insurer which is furnished at its request with rates and rating manuals by a rating organization of which it is not a member, or with advisory services by an advisory organization of which it is not a member.
3. (33-18-2) Title 33, Chapter 18, Section 2
"Subscriber" or "member" means a person to whom a subscription certificate is issued by a medical service corporation, which certificate sets forth the kinds and extent of the medical services for which the medical service corporation is liable to make payment [...]
4. (33-20-3) Title 33, Chapter 20, Section 3
"Subscriber" or "member" means a person to whom a subscriber's certificate is issued by a health care corporation, which certificate sets forth the kinds and extent of the health care services which may be all or a part of the total health care services used by [...]
5. (33-35-2) Title 33, Chapter 35, Section 2
(7) "Subscriber" means any person who has been enrolled in a prepaid legal services plan and is entitled to receive the benefits provided in the plan.
6. (36-90-2) Title 36, Chapter 90, Section 2
(13) "Subscriber" means any private person lawfully receiving any cable service provided by a private or public provider by means of or in connection with a cable system.
